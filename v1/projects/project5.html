Dragons: Wild Skies

Browser 3D Adventure

Dragons: Wild skies is an adventure game developed by indie developer Aquiris Game Studio, based on the Dreamworks movie franchise that brings the player to the island of the movies and gives them the unique hability to train your own dragon. On this project I worked on UI and icon assets.

The inventory sysem was designed to resonate the "Book of Dragons" that appears on the movie.
