NeueTech

Offline Reading for the Digital Age

NeueTech is a monthly magazine that focus on the best content in the world of technology. It's mission is give its readers the best of what's out in the endless information overflow that is the internet for in-depth reading sessions.

Examples of suggested magazine covers.

Gadgets reviews in vertical and horizontal lists.

Apps of the month and an editor's collum.

Special reports feature an unique double page layout.

For the branding of the magazine, each month would have a color of its own, taken from technology-based imagery.
